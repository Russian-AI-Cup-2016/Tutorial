 \bl 
 This attack damages all living units in a sector of {\tt -game.staffSector $/$ 2.0} to

 Magic missile is a basic spell of any wizard. Inflicts some damage upon a direct hit.
 \bl 
 The center of a newly created magic missile is the same as the center of a caster wizard. The angle of a
 projectile is equal to {\tt wizard.angle + move.castAngle}, and its speed is {\tt game.magicMissileSpeed}.
 All collisions between a projectile and its caster are ignored by the game engine.
 \bl 
 Requires {\tt game.magicMissileManacost} manapoints.}\end{itemize}
}
\item{
public static final ActionType FROST\_BOLT\begin{itemize}\item{\vskip -.9ex Cast a frost bolt.
 \bl 
 A frost bolt inflicts some damage upon a direct hit and freezes a target.
 \bl 
 The center of a newly created frost bolt is the same as the center of a caster wizard. The angle of a
 projectile is equal to {\tt wizard.angle + move.castAngle}, and its speed is {\tt game.frostBoltSpeed}.
 All collisions between a projectile and its caster are ignored by the game engine.
 \bl 
 Requires the {\tt FROST\_BOLT} skill and {\tt game.frostBoltManacost} manapoints.}\end{itemize}
}
\item{
public static final ActionType FIREBALL\begin{itemize}\item{\vskip -.9ex Cast a fireball.
 \bl 
 A fireball explodes when reaching maximal cast range or upon a collision with living unit.
 Damages and burns all living units nearby.
 \bl 
 The center of a newly created fireball is the same as the center of a caster wizard. The angle of a
 projectile is equal to {\tt wizard.angle + move.castAngle}, and its speed is {\tt game.fireballSpeed}.
 All collisions between a projectile and its caster are ignored by the game engine.
 \bl 
 Requires the {\tt FIREBALL} skill and {\tt game.fireballManacost} manapoints.}\end{itemize}
}
\item{
public static final ActionType HASTE\begin{itemize}\item{\vskip -.9ex Cast a haste spell, that temporarily speedups the friendly wizard with ID equal to {\tt move.statusTargetId}
 or the caster himself if the game engine can not find such wizard.
 \bl 
 Requires the {\tt HASTE} skill and {\tt game.hasteManacost} manapoints.}\end{itemize}
}
\item{
public static final ActionType SHIELD\begin{itemize}\item{\vskip -.9ex Cast a shield spell, that temporarily protects the friendly wizard with ID equal to {\tt move.statusTargetId}
 or the caster himself if the game engine can not find such wizard.
 \bl 
 Requires the {\tt SHIELD} skill and {\tt game.shieldManacost} manapoints.}\end{itemize}
}
